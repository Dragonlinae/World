Factions

Sins

Led by Pride, the sins seek to promote self-indulgence, focusing on individual rather than the group.

Neutrals

Neither completely sinful nor completely virtuous, they just want the two opposing factions to reconcile with each other.

Virtues

Led by Humility, the virtues oppose the sins, believing in always working to help others around them.
